Your Fit, Guided

Welcome to the Weavers Fit Studio. A precise, architectural approach to bespoke tailoring. Choose your preferred method to begin crafting your unique silhouette.

Method 01

Body Measurements

Follow our clean, anatomical step-by-step guide to capture your true physical dimensions. Ideal for first-time commissions requiring absolute precision.

Method 02

Garment Measurements

Replicate the fit of a piece you already own. Lay it flat and input the dimensions.

Method 03

Video Fitting

Connect live with a Master Tailor from our atelier. We will guide your tape measure in real-time.
