01. Introduction

Weavers Atelier (referred to as "the Atelier", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clientele. This document outlines how we manage personal information and the terms governing the purchase of our handcrafted textiles.

By engaging with our services, navigating our digital space, or commissioning a bespoke piece, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described herein. We reserve the right to refine these terms as our craft evolves.

02. Data Collection

PURPOSEFUL ACQUISITION

We collect only the information necessary to fulfill your orders and provide a personalized experience. This includes identity data, contact details, and financial transaction information processed through secure, encrypted gateways.

THE DIGITAL FOOTPRINT

Like the subtle patterns in our weave, we use cookies to understand how you interact with our atelier. This data is used solely to enhance site performance and provide relevant aesthetic inspiration.

03. Bespoke Terms

"Every thread is a commitment."

Bespoke commissions represent a unique collaboration. Once a design is finalized and weaving commences, cancellations are not permitted due to the custom nature of the materials and labor involved.

  • 01 A non-refundable deposit of 50% is required for all bespoke commissions.
  • 02 Lead times are estimates based on artisanal capacity and material sourcing.
  • 03 Slight variations in dye and weave are characteristics of natural fibers and hand-looming.

04. Shipping & Customs

We ship our textiles globally with white-glove couriers to ensure they reach you in pristine condition. All orders are insured against transit damage.

INTERNATIONAL DUTIES

Please be advised that for deliveries outside the European Union, the recipient is responsible for all import duties, customs fees, and local taxes. We recommend consulting your local customs office for estimated costs prior to finalizing a purchase.
